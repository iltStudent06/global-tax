Quarterly Tax Submission

Every company location is requested to fill out a tax questionnaire on a quarterly basis.

On this form, we are collecting the information below:

How much pre-tax income your operation will make for the whole year
How much tax your operation expects to pay
Any big changes that could affect taxes later

By submitting the tax questionnaire, you are confirming that the information provided is true and correct.

The Corporate Tax team will be using the information provided to calculate Liberty Mutual's overall tax rate and prepare quarterly financial reports.

Please click 'Next' to start filling out the questionnaire.
